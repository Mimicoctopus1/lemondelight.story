The Lemon Delight Story

Hello there!
Our lemonade stand is family owned and offers seasonal drinks.
Our recipe has a balance of freshly squeezed lemon juice, water, and
sugar to bring out the sweetness from the fruits.
The right way to set up a lemonade stand is to make signs
on cardboard or any sturdy surface and put them up close to your
location to attract people to buy drinks. You might want to sell
your lemonade for a low price so people will want to buy it. Use
to make your own signs and create your own free account.
Have fun!
